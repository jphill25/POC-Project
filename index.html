EV Efficiency Ratios

Below is a comparison of vehicle efficiency between driving on the highway and driving in the city. The catagories are generalized vehicle data, ranging from subcompact to SUV's. We can see that overall, the gas vehicles have a very good fuel efficiency and still outperform EV's. The most important part to note is the high efficiency on the highway, as EV's outperform gsa vehicles over short distances.
